How much water should I drink daily?

+

The general recommendation is to drink 8 glasses (about 2 liters) of water per day. However, individual needs vary based on:

  • Body weight and size
  • Physical activity level
  • Climate and temperature
  • Overall health condition

Listen to your body and drink when you're thirsty. Your urine color is a good indicator - it should be light yellow.

What is a balanced diet?

+

A balanced diet includes a variety of foods from all major food groups:

  • Fruits and Vegetables: 5 servings daily for vitamins and fiber
  • Whole Grains: Brown rice, whole wheat, oats for energy
  • Proteins: Lean meat, fish, eggs, legumes for muscle health
  • Dairy: Milk, yogurt, cheese for calcium and bone health
  • Healthy Fats: Nuts, seeds, olive oil in moderation

Limit processed foods, sugar, and excessive salt intake.

How often should I exercise?

+

The World Health Organization recommends:

  • Adults (18-64): At least 150 minutes of moderate-intensity aerobic activity per week
  • Or: 75 minutes of vigorous-intensity activity per week
  • Muscle-strengthening: Activities 2 or more days per week
  • Break it down: 30 minutes, 5 days a week is achievable

Include activities like walking, jogging, swimming, cycling, or dancing.

Why is sleep important for health?

+

Quality sleep is crucial for overall health and wellbeing:

  • Physical Health: Repairs tissues, strengthens immune system
  • Mental Health: Improves mood, reduces stress and anxiety
  • Cognitive Function: Enhances memory, concentration, and decision-making
  • Weight Management: Regulates hunger hormones
  • Disease Prevention: Reduces risk of chronic conditions

Aim for 7-9 hours of quality sleep each night for adults.

How can I boost my immune system?

+

Strengthen your immune system naturally with these habits:

  • Nutrition: Eat vitamin C-rich foods (citrus, berries) and zinc sources (nuts, seeds)
  • Exercise: Regular moderate exercise enhances immune function
  • Sleep: Get 7-9 hours to allow immune cell regeneration
  • Stress Management: Practice meditation, yoga, or deep breathing
  • Hygiene: Wash hands regularly, maintain cleanliness
  • Hydration: Drink plenty of water to flush toxins

What are signs of dehydration?

+

Common signs of dehydration include:

  • Dark yellow or amber-colored urine
  • Feeling thirsty or dry mouth
  • Fatigue and dizziness
  • Headaches
  • Dry skin and lips
  • Decreased urination
  • Rapid heartbeat

If you experience severe symptoms like confusion or rapid breathing, seek medical attention immediately.

How can I reduce stress naturally?

+

Natural stress-relief techniques include:

  • Deep Breathing: Practice 4-7-8 breathing technique
  • Exercise: Physical activity releases endorphins
  • Meditation: 10-15 minutes daily mindfulness practice
  • Nature Time: Spend time outdoors in green spaces
  • Social Connection: Talk to friends and family
  • Hobbies: Engage in activities you enjoy
  • Limit Caffeine: Reduce coffee and energy drinks

What vitamins should I take daily?

+

Essential vitamins for daily health (best from food, supplements if needed):

  • Vitamin D: For bone health and immunity (sunlight, fatty fish)
  • Vitamin C: For immune support (citrus fruits, peppers)
  • B Vitamins: For energy production (whole grains, eggs)
  • Vitamin A: For eye health (carrots, sweet potatoes)
  • Vitamin E: For antioxidant protection (nuts, seeds)

Consult a healthcare provider before starting any supplement regimen.
